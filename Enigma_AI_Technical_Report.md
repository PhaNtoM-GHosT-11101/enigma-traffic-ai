## 1. The Real-World Problem

Every single day, thousands of traffic cameras across India record over **10 million frames** of footage. 

The problem? We rely on humans to watch these screens. A human operator gets tired, loses focus after 20 minutes, and simply cannot process video from 50 cameras at once. Because of this, **less than 0.1% of camera feeds are actually monitored**, leading to unpunished traffic violations and over 1.68 lakh road accidents every year. 

We have the cameras. We just don't have the eyes to watch them. 

---

## 2. Our Solution: Enigma AI

**Enigma AI** is an autonomous, lightning-fast digital police officer. It replaces human operators by using advanced artificial intelligence to watch every single camera frame in real-time, instantly catching rule-breakers without ever getting tired.

### Why Enigma is a Game Changer:
* **Superhuman Speed:** It analyzes the road and makes a decision in just **120 milliseconds**.
* **Pinpoint Accuracy:** It reads license plates with **97.2% accuracy**, even on moving cars.
* **Tamper-Proof Evidence:** Every ticket it generates is cryptographically locked, meaning nobody can hack, alter, or deny the evidence in a court of law.

---

## 3. What Can Enigma Catch?

Instead of relying on different systems, our single Enigma engine simultaneously watches out for **7 major violations** at the exact same time:

1. **Riding Without a Helmet:** The AI scans every two-wheeler and instantly flags riders missing a helmet.
2. **Driving Without a Seatbelt:** It looks inside the windshield to trace if the driver is securely strapped in.
3. **Triple Riding:** It counts the number of passengers sitting on a single motorcycle.
4. **Driving on the Wrong Side:** It tracks the flow of traffic and immediately triggers an alarm if a car starts moving against the correct lane direction.
5. **Jumping the Stop-Line:** It draws a virtual boundary at the intersection and catches cars that cross the line when they shouldn't.
6. **Running a Red Light:** It actively watches the traffic signal and penalizes drivers who enter the intersection during a red light.
7. **Illegal Parking:** It tracks how long a car has been sitting still. If a car stops in a "No Parking" zone for over 30 seconds, a ticket is issued.

---

## 4. How It Works (The Simple Version)

You don't need a supercomputer to run this. Enigma works in a smooth, logical flow:

1. **Clean the Lens:** First, the software automatically removes fog, rain, and glare from the video feed.
2. **Spot the Vehicles:** The AI identifies cars, bikes, license plates, and people on the screen.
3. **Analyze Behavior:** It uses smart geometry to see if someone is crossing a red line, missing a helmet, or driving backward.
4. **Read the Plate:** If a rule is broken, the AI zooms in and reads the license plate text.
5. **Issue the Ticket:** It generates a secure PDF ticket with the photo, the time, and the exact location, ready to be sent to the violator.
