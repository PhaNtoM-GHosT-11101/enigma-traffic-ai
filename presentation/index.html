Risks & MITIGATION

[ BOTTLENECK_DETECTED ]

Hardware Limits

Risk: CPU-only processing limits throughput to 5-8 FPS, causing queue backlog.

Mitigation: Deployed via FastAPI on edge NVIDIA hardware (Jetson Orin Nano). Implemented dynamic frame-skipping (processing 1 in 5 frames) during extreme traffic surges to prevent memory overflow.

[ ENV_NOISE_DETECTED ]

Environmental Noise

Risk: Heavy monsoon rain, fog, or mud obscure license plates, confusing the OCR.

Mitigation: Pre-processing via OpenCV CLAHE boosts contrast. Failed regex matches automatically flag the docket as "Unreadable Plate" routing it to a human supervisor instead of discarding the evidence.
